Sherrie Cohen

Democratic Candidate for JUDGE OF THE MUNICIPAL COURT

Sherrie Cohen | Photo by Colleen Stepanian

Sherrie Cohen is an elected judge of the Philadelphia Municipal Court. She is a progressive activist for the Philadelphia public library system, the rights of tenants, and the LGBTQ+. She has stated in a PhillyMag interview, “rec centers are in need of more capital funding to restore them... Our library system is in need of more funding for more resources and more staff and more programs.”
